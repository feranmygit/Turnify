Turnify revolutionizes queue management, giving your customers seamless experiences and saving valuable time

Features

Flexible Queue Access

Allow customers to join queues remotely through their devices or onsite at your location, ensuring convenience and accessibility for everyone

Real-Time Updates

Keep your customers informed with real-time queue updates directly to their devices

Time-Based Control

Set a specific time window for enabling or disabling remote joining of the queue. Easily control when customers can join the queue online

Real-Time Notifications

Notify staff in real time when someone joins the queue remotely, ensuring seamless communication and readiness

Hassle-Free Queue Cancellation

Empower remote customers to cancel their spot in the queue easily if they can't make it, keeping the system efficient and fair for everyone

Data-Driven Queue Analytics

Leverage powerful analytics to monitor and optimize your queue system. Gain insights into wait times, peak periods, and customer flow to enhance efficiency and improve customer experience

Why Choose Turnify?

Prevent queue jumping by enforcing a strict, first-come, first-served system that ensures fairness and order for all customers
